Brownies

Yummy fudgy chocolate brownies to satisfy your sweet tooth.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 1/2 all-purpose flour
  • 2 sticks melted butter
  • 4 Eggs
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 salt
  • 1/2 cup chopped walnuts

Directions

      Step 1

      Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F(175 degrees C). Grease a 9-inch pan.

      Step 2

      Mix all the ingredients in a bowl and mix, add in the chopped walnuts. spread the batter into the prepared pan.

      Step 3

      Bake in the preheated oven until top is dry and edges have started to pull away form the sides of the pan, about 20 tp 30 minutes, cool and dry before slicing into squares.
