Chocolate And Chip Scones

A combination of sweet and savory flavors packed in our hearty buttery smooth scones with melting chocolate chips.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cups white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 5 tablespoon unsalted butter, chilled and cubed
  • 1/2 cup miniature semisweet chocolate chips
  • 3 tablespoon orange juice, or more if needed

Directions

      Step 1

      Preheat the oven to over 400 degrees F(200 degrees C). Spray a baking sheet with nonstick cooking spray.

      Step 2

      Whisk flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt together in a large bowl. Cut in butter with pastry blender or a large fork until the mixture resembels coarse crumbs. Stir in chocolate chips. Mix in orange juice to form a dough.

      Step 3

      Turn out dough on a floured surface. Pat and roll intoa 9-inch about 1/2 inch thick. With 2 1/2-inch fluted buscuit cutter , cut out 12 scones, Pushing the dough scraps togethr for the last few, if necessary. Trnasfer the sconrs to the baking sheet

      Step 4

      Bake in preheated oven until golden brown, about 12 minutes. Move to wire racks to cool.
